About

I received the B.E. degree from Central South University in 2018 and the Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Guihua Zeng (曾贵华) in 2023. From Oct. 2023, I have joined Shanghai Jiao Tong University as an Assistant Professor.

My work focuses on using modern AI techniques to address frontier problems in quantum science, including efficient learning of quantum systems, quantum circuit optimization, error mitigation and correction, and quantum-computing-based AI algorithms for sensing and imaging.

Research

Quantum Artificial Intelligence

Learning, control, optimization, and generative modeling for quantum systems and programmable quantum circuits.

Intelligent Quantum Sensing

Machine-learning-assisted parameter estimation, adaptive sensing, and reinforcement-learning strategies near fundamental limits.

AI-Enhanced Optical Computing and Imaging

Quantum and neural approaches to ghost imaging, optical modulation, computational imaging, and scattering-media reconstruction.
