About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Central South University in 2018, and the Doctor degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, in 2023.
From 2023.10, I have joined Shanghai Jiao Tong University as an Assistant Professor.

As a dedicated professional in the field of Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), I am committed to leveraging advanced AI technologies to address frontier quantum problems such as how to efficiently learn quantum systems, how to acclerate quantum circuit optimization, error mitigation and correction. Besides, I am also conducting research on quantum computing based AI algorithms such as quantum-enhanced machine learning algorithms and their applications in quantum sensing and image processing.
